Jaqi Loye-Brown

This independent publisher is breaking all the romantic fiction rules in conjunction with MT-Ink Publishing. The mission: to smash up the same old story mould. Functioning novellas on the periphery of polite publishing.

 

We are an emerging label whose titles won't spare the hoaxes, the unsaid, a series of truth-hoods and calling out all manner of social norms and worship culture.
